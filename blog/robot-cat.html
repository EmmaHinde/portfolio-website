Robot Cat

2021

In my final year of high school, I started making a small quadruped robot out of craft sticks, micro-servos, and an arduino. As you might expect, it didn't work very well. I only managed to get two legs moving on the prototype, but I did learn some inverse kinematics to model the gait pattern for the motors. It didn't work because I didn't have enough power to the servos.
