About Me

A quick overview of who I am and what I do.

Hi, I'm Emma! I'm a Mechatronics and Robotics Engineer with a passion for making cool stuff and working across engineering disciplines to bring projects together. This is a place to stick some of the projects I've worked on, and have a bit of a blog sorta thing going on in case anyone else turns up and runs into the same problems.

I'm really excited about the potential for robotics in dangerous or hard to reach locations for environmental and sustainibility purposes. Also bio-inspired robots are so cool! This has got a bunch of random projects from uni ones to internship ones to random personal things I did years ago and am writing up from what I can remember.

When I'm not doing robotics projects, I run art events for other students and am working towards getting the Uni of Leeds Astronomy society access to the old physics domes.
